Definition: A stage in cell division when chromatin condenses into discrete chromosomes. The nuclear envelope breaks down and spindle fibers form at opposite "poles" of the cell. Prophase

 Prophase is a beautiful menagerie of changes that occur in both the cytoplasm and nucleus of a dividing cell.
