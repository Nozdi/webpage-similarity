If you are interested in science and enjoy math, you have many education and career options. Have you considered engineering? In chemistry, for example, what is the difference between a chemist and a chemical engineer? In theory, a chemist may be more focused on pure research and a chemical engineer on the application of chemistry to solve real-life problems. In practice, these two professions may do much the same thing. However, the average starting salary for a chemical engineer is much higher than that of a chemist, plus the engineer is more likely to find a job right out of school. Choosing a career is about more than money and employability, but for most people these are strong factors... more reasons to be an engineer
