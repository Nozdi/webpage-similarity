* Greek Mythology

 Greek gods and goddesses were not benign unseen eternal and omniscient entities, they were often shaped like men and women, with human appearing emotions, and often interacting with humans. * Roman Gods and Goddesses

 While the Romans did adopt many of the Greek gods and goddesses, there were plenty of uniquely Roman ones, as well as other spirits and numina. These are lists of the Romans' gods divided into categories. * The Stories of Gods and Men

 Many of the ancient Greek myths tell stories about mortal Greek heroes aided by their gods.
