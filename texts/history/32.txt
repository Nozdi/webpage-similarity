Hyperborea - About Hyperborea

Hyperborea is a mythical place -- an island, sometimes -- in the distant north, sometimes specified as to the north of Thrace. Its residents are the Hyperboreans. The name Hyperborea was thought to mean beyond Boreas, or beyond the North Wind, since Boreas was the name of the North Wind.
