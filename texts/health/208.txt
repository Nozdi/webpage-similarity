I sure couldn't fool anyone with last week's word "dementia". Everyone who tried the quiz go the right answer. Dementia is an organic brain disorder. It refers basically to the symptom not the cause. There is progressive deterioration of mental function. The main features are disorientation, memory loss and possible loss of contact with reality. Various disease processes, such as Alzheimer's, can cause dementia. The late stages of syphilis and psychoses can also be responsible for these mental changes. The actual disease causing the dementia would determine treatment.
