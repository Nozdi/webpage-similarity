Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)

Pelvic inflammatory disease affects millions of women each year in the United States and is an infection of one or more pelvic organs, including the uterus, cervix, and fallopian tubes. Learn the recognize the symptoms of this potentially life threatening STD, and find out what treatments are available.
