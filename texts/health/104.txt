It's important to keep the area affected by genital herpes dry and clean. Try not to touch the area and always wash your hands if you have touched the area affected by genital herpes. You should avoid sexual intercourse from the time you feel symptoms until the sores have healed to prevent spreading genital herpes to your partner.
