Definition: A sexually transmitted disease caused by type 2 herpes virus. Genital herpes usually presents with painful blisters; other symptoms can include fever, headaches, and muscle aches. First-time outbreaks usually appear about 6 days after exposure. Although treatment is available to make recurrent outbreaks less severe, there is no cure.

Also Known As: HSV, herpes simplex virus, genital blisters, vaginal sores
