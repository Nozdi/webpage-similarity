Book Port DT is a desktop-style Digital Talking Book player and recorder produced by the American Printing House for the Blind.

Like other book players such as the Victor Reader Stream, the Book Port DT supports a range of audio formats, including DAISY books.

The device also enables users to record or edit their own content in digital audio format, listen to Internet radio stations, and download podcasts.

The Book Port DT has a large speaker, easy-to-use tactile controls, features both computer and Internet connectivity, and supports Bluetooth technologies.
