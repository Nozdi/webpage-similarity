Role-playing games and the iPad tend to go hand-in-hand.  While some game genres, like first-person shooters, can come off awkward on a touch device if not done just right, RPGs tend to fall in line with the iPad's mechanics.  The best RPGs on the iPad tend to use a touch-to-walk scheme, but as games like Dungeon Hunters show, on-screen movement pads can work just fine.
