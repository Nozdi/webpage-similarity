Sugarbush Ski Resort is located in Warren, Vermont, with 111 ski trails, served by 16 lifts. Sugarbush offers lessons and programs for kids and adults, as well as specialty programs for racers and backcountry skiers. Sugarbush Resort is known for its excellent terrain.



Directions Sugarbush Resort is located in Warren, Vermont, 45 minutes from Burlington, VT, 3 hours from Boston, MA, and 5 hours from New York City.
