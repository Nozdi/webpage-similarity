Did you know that you can obtain some pretty good racquetball instruction, from world-class coaches, right on your computer via the Web?Coaches do need to be paid so they can earn a living, but all of the following coaches are kind enough to offer excellent advice (or at least some samples and book excerpts) for free on their websites:Take a look at:Carl Moody's Racquetball Instruction site,the Racquetball Academy offered by Jo Shattuck,Dennis Fisher's Racquetball University , andFran Davis's instructional videos You should also take a look at the instructional books and videos created by Jim Winterton . Although you may not be able to find free excerpts of these on the Web, the books are well-regarded offerings from an accomplished coach.Until you line up lessons with your regular racquetball coach, enjoy these online alternatives and study up!
