One of the best ways to take a quantum leap in your racquet sports play is to participate in a camp. There are several racquetball camps you might consider, like Fran Davis's weekend or week-long camps , the week-long high performance camp held at the US Olympic training center (here's a camp sampler ), or Jo Shattuck's shorter clinics If you have the time and money, and decide to attend a racquetball camp, be sure to do a few things in addition to making your travel and lodging reservations:When you get back, work hard to incorporate your new-found skills and drills, and then use what you learned in play with your regular partners. They'll be wanting to attend the camp next time!
