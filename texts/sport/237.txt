Snowshoe Mountain Ski Area, located in Snowshoe, West Virginia, has 60 ski trails, served by 14 lifts and is the largest winter resort in the mid-Atlantic and Southeast.



 Directions Snowshoe Mountain is located in Snowshoe, West Virginia, 168 miles from Charleston, WV and 241 miles from Washington, DC.



Lift Tickets / Season Passes Lift tickets, season passes, children's lesson programs, and weekly passes can be purchased online.
