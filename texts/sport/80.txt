A rookie NYC cop has been reassigned to desk duty after knocking a Critical Mass cyclist off his bike during a ride through Times Square Friday night with a hit that would make an NFL linebacker proud.

And get this: it was the cyclist was charged with attempted assault, resisting arrest, and disorderly conduct.

Tension has been high between the cops and Critical Massers ever since the 2004 Republican National Convention. Unfortunately this just seems to be another black mark against the Gotham City cops in their treatment of cyclists.
