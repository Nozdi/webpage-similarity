

 Mike's Bromley Mountain Photo Gallery Bromley Mountain is located in Peru, Vermont, with 45 ski trails, served by 10 lifts. Bromley Mountain boasts being the only mountain in Vermont with southern exposure.



 Directions Bromley Mountain is located on Route 11, in Peru, Vermont, 6 miles outside of Manchester, VT, 3 hours from Boston, MA., and 4 hours from New York City.



Lift Tickets / Season Passes Season passes, ticket deals and adult and children's lesson programs are available.
