Shortcrust pastry is so easy to make. It is one of the most versatile pastry as it can be used for both savory and sweet dishes. Shortcrust pastry is a mixture of flour - usually all purpose or plain flour - as it is known in Britain - and a fat, either butter, lard or a mixture of both. These are bound together with either cold water or with egg for a richer pastry. There is no need to be afraid of making pastry, it really is so easy. The pastry can be made either by hand or for speed you can use a food processor if you have one.
