The season for fresh season asparagus is short, so make the most of it while it is available. There are many ways to cook and serve asparagus but they don't come easier than Asparagus Twigs, Baked Asparagus in Filo Pastry.Asparagus Twigs make a great canape for parties and picnic s, but equally make a great starter dish served with a side salad and mayonnaise for dipping.
