Khasta means 'crispy' in Hindi. Delicious Khasta Kachori or Khasta Kachori is a popular snack in Northern and Central India. One bite and you will see why! This popular North Indian snack can be found at most Indian cafes. Make a batch and keep them in an air-tight container for upto a week. Simply reheat in your oven when you are ready to snack on some! The Moong Daal for the filling needs to be pre-soaked so keep that in mind for prep time.
