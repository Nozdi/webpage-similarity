Cream of Asparagus soup is a great way to use up misshapen bundles of Asparagus as misshapen bundles cost less than picture perfect ones, so you can save money too. This soup is lovely in early spring, when asparagus is in season, and also makes a lovely cold soup on a warm summers' day.British asparagus soup is best during the brief but splendid British Asparagus Season which runs from late April to late June.
