I altered almost all of these recipes so they made the five ingredient cut. This isn't that difficult! Many crockpot recipes can be altered to include fewer ingredients with no loss in flavor or quality. Just make sure that the recipe ingredients you eliminate aren't essential, like the broth in a soup or binders in meatloaf. For tips on crockpot use, see Crockpot Lessons.
