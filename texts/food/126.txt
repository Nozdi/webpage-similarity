Definition: Lentils. These are some of the more common kinds: Masoor (split red lentils)  These are actually orange-colored grains. The whole variety with the skin still on is pale brown in colour. Urad (black gram) - Available with or without its black outer skin. Inside the black skin is a cream-colored lentil. Chana (large split yellow lentil) - Looks like a larger version of Moong.

As in Maa ki daal, Daal fry and Masala daal.
