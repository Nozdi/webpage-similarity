Question: What is the purpose of the About Dogs website?  Answer: The About Dogs website is dedicated to promoting Responsible Dog Ownership throughout the world. Educating the average dog owner about the responsibilities of being a dog owner. To teach about the proper care of Man's Best Friend. 

Devoted to helping new owners, and soon-to-be dog owners, make the right choices for their present and future pets. Making the right choices now reduces the chances of future heartache caused by behaviour problems and health issues, and results in a happy, healthy, companion for the life of your dog. Help your dog live her life to the fullest by taking proper care with her health and well-being!
