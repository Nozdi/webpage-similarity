One thing I often hear when talking cars is "I'd love to buy a small fuel-efficient car, but..." There are lots of perceived reasons that people can't live with a small car. In response to ever-increasing gas prices, automakers have turned their attention to designing smaller, more fuel-efficient cars that address the needs of all vehicle owners -- those seeking lots of space for cargo or passengers, those who want high-end luxury gizmos, and, of course, those who simply don't want to spend a lot of money.
