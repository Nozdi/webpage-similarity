Learning certain categories of words is critical to becoming a better Scrabble player. Knowing these vowel-heavy five-letter words can help when you're stuck with a rack full of vowels. All of these words have at least four vowels. You might also want to learn Scrabble's legal two-letter words and words with a Q but no U. Here are all of our Scrabble word lists, and some simple ways to improve your Scrabble play. NOTE: These words are legal according to the Official Scrabble Players Dictionary, 4th Edition. They may not be legal if you use a different dictionary.
