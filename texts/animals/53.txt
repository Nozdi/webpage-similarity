Bring a puppy into a room full of people, and the majority of them will want to get a closer look. If nothing else, you will bring smiles to many faces. Regardless of breed or history, puppies are universally adorable. Is it those big, cute eyes and instant affection? Perhaps it is the puppy breath and kisses. Or, maybe it is their sheer youth and innocence. In truth, it's probably all of the above. Whatever it is, it is temporary, fleeting and - for many of us - irresistible.
