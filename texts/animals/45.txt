Question: I lost my dog! What do I do?

Answer: Don't panic!



 Losing your dog is heartbreaking and many of us tend to panic at the "what to do?" point. Take a deep bereath, and approach this methodically so that you don't forget something important. 



 Here is a list of things you should be doing: Help, My Dog is Missing!. 



 And here is a Printable Checklist, to help you keep track of what has been done : Lost Dog Checklist. 



 Keep your head about you, don't panic, and most important of all : Don't Give Up!
