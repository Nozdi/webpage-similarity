Where Special Needs Families Care and Share Together.

Our Special Families Village is for families who have members with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities. A "one stop shop" to make finding information and resources as easy as clicking your mouse.

Here you will find information, resources, and support on topics that matter to our Families - Special Education, Special Needs Financial Planning, Medical Information and Resources, Parenting Support, Information on Speech and Language Therapy, Behavior Therapy and a whole lot more.

Stop on by http://www.OurSpecialFamiliesVillage.org, we look forward to meeting you!
