Listed here are Art History words and terms that begin with the letter "N". Selecting any entry below will lead to its page and the ability to view its full definition.

negative space

Negative space is a compositional tool used in both two- and three-dimensional work. The simplest way to describe it is as: "space where other things are not present."

Netherlandish

Of or pertaining to the Netherlands. In art history, Netherlandish refers to art created in present day Belgium and The Netherlands (also known as "Holland") before 1830.

nonobjective art

A work of art that does not represent or depict a person, place or thing in the natural world.

nonrepresentational art

An artwork that does not represent or depict a being, place or thing in the natural world.
